Majors

  • The mathematics curriculum provides a rigorous foundation in theoretical and applied mathematics.
  • The computer science curriculum provides a broad overview of computer science as a discipline and gives the student essential technical and analytical skills.
  • The data science curriculum weaves together traditional curriculum in statistics, mathematics and computer science along with curriculum related to current data science techniques.
  • The cybersecurity curriculum, in concert with the core curriculum, helps fulfill the undergraduate mission of the university by preparing students for challenging and meaningful careers that aid in cyber defense.

Double Majors

A unique aspect to our programs is the ability to double major without overloading semesters. There is overlap between content of the programs and several courses can be double counted. For a student who majors in two of our programs, the number of credits in any given semester is at most 16. Students can double major in: Computer Science and Cybersecurity (64 credits), Computer Science and Data Science (65 credits), Data Science and Mathematics (65 credits), Data Science and Cybersecurity (67 credits), Computer Science and Mathematics (69 credits).

Source: Mount St. Mary's Course Catalog

Emmitsburg, MD

Mount St. Mary's University is located along Rt. 15 in Emmitsburg, MD. Emmitsburg is a small town with great charm and many attractions. First populated in the early 1700s, the town was officially founded in 1785. Some of the recommended things to see are the Emmitsburg Disc Golf Course, the Frederick County Fire and Rescue Museum, the National Shrine of St. Elizabeth Ann Seton, the National Shrine Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes, and the National Fallen Firefighters Memorial.

Source: Visit Emmitsburg
